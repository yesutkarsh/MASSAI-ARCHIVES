Why Vocabulary and Pronunciation?
- Strong vocabulary and clear pronunciation helps to express ideas accurately and make a lasting impression.


Vocabulary Building Technique:
- Learn as much as word can by reading or searching
- Do Not use word of which meaning is not clear to you.


Pronunciation Improvement
- Break it down in small chunks like pro - nun - ciation
- Shadowing Technique, Mimic native speaker's rhythm
- Put stress on words.


Final takeaway
- Learn new word daily
- Set 10 min Reading session.
